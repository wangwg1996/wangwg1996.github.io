Biography

 I am now a third-year Ph.D. student at the School of Software, Tsinghua University. I work in the Systems and Ubiquitous Networking (SUN) group in the Institute of Trustworthy Networks and Systems, advised by Prof. Yuan He. My research interests include wireless sensing and communication, mobile and ubiquitous computing.
